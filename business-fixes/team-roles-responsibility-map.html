THE BUSINESS PROBLEM

What this fixes

Employees are busy, but routine decisions still return to the owner. Responsibilities overlap, tasks fall between roles, and team members do not know what they can decide, when they must escalate, or who owns the next step.

IDEAL CUSTOMER

Who this is built for

Owner-led businesses with 2–25 employees, recurring handoff problems, repeated owner interruptions, or roles that have grown informally without being clarified.

EXPECTED OUTCOME

What changes

The team can see who owns recurring work, who may make routine decisions, where handoffs occur, and when an issue truly requires the owner.

DELIVERABLES

What 3P provides

  • A role inventory for up to eight roles or recurring responsibility groups.
  • A responsibility matrix covering up to 25 recurring duties, decisions, and handoffs.
  • Decision-right and escalation rules showing what each role can decide and when the owner is needed.
  • One-page team responsibility map suitable for staff use.
  • A list of overlaps, gaps, single-person dependencies, and immediate clarification actions.
  • A 45-minute manager walkthrough and one revision round.
CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

What you provide

  • Ensure the owner or manager can explain current authority and escalation expectations.
  • Resolve internal disagreements about final accountability.
  • Provide one consolidated revision response within two business days.
