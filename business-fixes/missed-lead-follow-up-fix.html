THE BUSINESS PROBLEM

What this fixes

Calls, website requests, emails, texts, and social messages arrive through different places. The owner intends to respond, but the next action lives in memory, a notebook, or a crowded inbox. Good leads cool off because no one can quickly see what has been answered, quoted, scheduled, or lost.

IDEAL CUSTOMER

Who this is built for

Owner-operated businesses with 1–10 employees, two or more inquiry channels, and no dependable shared view of open leads and follow-up.

EXPECTED OUTCOME

What changes

The business has one visible list of active inquiries. Each lead has a clear status, named owner, next action, and follow-up date so opportunities are no longer dependent on memory.

DELIVERABLES

What 3P provides

  • One customized lead and follow-up tracker built in a practical spreadsheet format.
  • Lead stages, source, contact status, assigned owner, next action, follow-up date, estimated value, and notes.
  • A clear follow-up cadence for new inquiries, unanswered quotes, and no-response leads.
  • One missed-call response template and three customer follow-up templates.
  • Import of up to 100 active leads from one clean client-provided spreadsheet.
  • A 30-minute implementation walkthrough and one revision round.
