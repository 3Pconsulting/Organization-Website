THE BUSINESS PROBLEM

What this fixes

Quotes are created in one tool, discussed in another, and followed up only when someone remembers. The owner cannot quickly see which estimates are still open, which customers need an answer, or how much potential work is sitting untouched.

IDEAL CUSTOMER

Who this is built for

Trades, repair businesses, contractors, professional services, and local businesses sending recurring quotes or estimates without a consistent aging and follow-up process.

EXPECTED OUTCOME

What changes

Open estimates are visible by age, value, owner, and next action. The business can follow up consistently and identify delayed quotes before the customer moves on.

DELIVERABLES

What 3P provides

  • One customized quote and estimate tracker with open-value and aging visibility.
  • Quote stages, sent date, age, amount, owner, next action, follow-up date, and final outcome.
  • A simple quote-turnaround standard and follow-up cadence.
  • Three quote follow-up templates for recent, aging, and final-contact situations.
  • Import of up to 100 open or recently closed quotes from one clean client-provided spreadsheet.
  • A 30-minute implementation walkthrough and one revision round.

  • Attend the walkthrough and provide revision feedback within two business days.
