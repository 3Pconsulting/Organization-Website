How pricing works

Defined projects are quoted by scope. Cleanup sprints are priced around the volume and condition of the work. Recurring support uses a monthly scope with stated capacity, responsibilities, and limits.

No unlimited work. No surprise expansion. Additional needs are discussed before they become additional charges.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Before you start

Can 3P replace an employee?

3P can absorb defined operational work and recurring responsibilities, but it is not a substitute for every type of employee. The goal is to determine whether the business truly needs a hire, needs temporary capacity, or first needs a better process.

Can we start with one small project?

Yes. A focused project is often the best way to establish what the business needs and whether broader support would be useful.

Will I need to buy new software?

Not automatically. 3P first looks at what you already use. New technology is recommended only when it solves a defined need and the value justifies the cost.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. Most support can be delivered remotely through shared files, video calls, email, and approved access to the systems required for the work.
