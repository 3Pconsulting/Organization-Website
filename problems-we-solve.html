HOW 3P THINKS

We do not treat every problem as a software problem or a people problem.

A process may fail because expectations are unclear. A person may struggle because the system makes the right action difficult. A product or service may be unprofitable because the workflow around it consumes more labor than anyone measures.

3P looks across People, Process, and Product so the fix addresses the business as a whole.

A useful fix should do at least one of four things:

  • Return time
  • Protect revenue or margin
  • Reduce errors and risk
  • Improve the customer or employee experience
