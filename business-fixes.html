Yes — real operational consulting, from someone who has done this work at a high level. The difference is how it's delivered: a defined service with a specific deliverable, price, timeline, and completion point, not open-ended hourly billing.

Will I need new software?

Not automatically. 3P uses the simplest workable tool and does not recommend new software until the process and actual requirement are clear.

Can the scope be expanded?

Yes, but added workflows, departments, records, integrations, training, or ongoing work are quoted separately before they begin.

How quickly can you start?

After payment, complete intake, required files, access, and client decisions are received.

What happens after delivery?

You receive the final system, instructions, walkthrough, and a recommendation only when a larger fix or ongoing support is justified.

What if I am not sure which fix I need?

Complete the Operational Snapshot. It routes mixed or unclear symptoms before you purchase the wrong service.
