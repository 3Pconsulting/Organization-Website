THE BUSINESS PROBLEM

What this fixes

The work is finished, but billing information arrives late, invoices are not sent promptly, and outstanding balances are followed up inconsistently. Revenue exists on paper but remains trapped between job completion, invoice creation, due dates, and payment.

IDEAL CUSTOMER

Who this is built for

Small service, repair, construction-support, healthcare-adjacent, and professional businesses that need clearer visibility from completed work through paid status.

EXPECTED OUTCOME

What changes

The owner can see what is complete but not billed, what has been invoiced, what is due, what is overdue, and who owns the next action.
