Owner Exercise

The 5-Minute Waste Check

Examine one recurring task and quickly identify waiting, rework, bottlenecks, unclear ownership, and unnecessary handoffs.

Choose one recurring task.

Use scheduling, quoting, ordering, invoicing, production, customer follow-up, or approvals. Do not assess the entire business at once. One process is enough to reveal the pattern.

Three or more checked boxes: The process is probably depending on memory, workarounds, or owner intervention instead of a reliable system.

Turn the check into action

Write down the current steps exactly as they happen—not how the procedure says they should happen. Mark every delay, correction, handoff, and approval. That is your starting map.
